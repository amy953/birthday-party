Common Questions

Good Questions.
Straight Answers.

Do you allow outside vendors?

Yes — bring your own caterer, photographer, florist, and DJ. We have a list of local vendors we love and are happy to share recommendations if you need them.

What happens if it rains?

We've got you covered — literally. We have an outdoor event tent available for rain protection, plus indoor overflow space adjacent to the pasture. We'll work with you in advance on a rain plan so your event stays on track no matter the weather.

Are the farm animals part of the event?

Our chickens and pigs are on the property and can be part of the experience if you'd like — or we can keep them in their areas so they stay out of your event space. Your call.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend inquiring at least 3–6 months ahead for weddings and large events. Smaller celebrations can sometimes be arranged with less lead time — just ask.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. A 50% deposit is required to hold your date. The remaining balance is due 30 days before your event. Deposits are non-refundable but dates can be moved with 60 days notice.

Can I host a yoga class or sound bath here regularly?

Yes — we offer a discounted recurring rate for yoga instructors, coaches, and facilitators who want to use the farm on a weekly or monthly basis. Reach out through the inquiry form and let us know your cadence and we'll put together a custom arrangement.

Are events only on weekends?

Not at all — both our outdoor pasture and large indoor event room are available any day of the week. Whether you're planning a weekday rehearsal dinner or a Sunday celebration, we can accommodate you.
